Unverifiable

When there is no way to know.

Many meaningful and important phenomena are resistant to scientific methodology. How do you conduct a controlled experiment on an international trade system? How do falsify a belief structure? How do you measure intimacy? This does not mean that they are irrelevant in a technological context and cannot be taken seriously, quite the opposite.
